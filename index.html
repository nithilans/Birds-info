The introduction of Birds

The introduction of birds says that they are an organization of Aves-class warm-blooded vertebrates characterized by wings, hard-shelled egg-laying, toothless beaked jaws, an increased metabolic rate, a heart with four chambers, and a powerful yet light skeleton. The bird's scientific name is Aves.
